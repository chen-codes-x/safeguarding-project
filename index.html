About SnapSafe Learning

SnapSafe Learning is a quick and engaging safeguarding refresher tool designed for trainee primary school teachers. Partnering with the NSPCC, it delivers bite-sized modules focused on children aged 4–7, helping future teachers recognise and respond to safeguarding concerns with confidence.

What is Safeguarding?

Safeguarding means keeping children safe from harm, abuse, and neglect by creating secure, supportive environments where pupils can thrive.
