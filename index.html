About SafeSnap Learning

SnapSafe Learning is a quick and engaging safeguarding refresher tool designed for trainee primary school teachers. Partnering with the NSPCC, it delivers bite-sized modules focused on children aged 4–7, making safeguarding knowledge clear, memorable, and easy to apply. Designed to be used as a training day activity or informal deliverable, it includes a short end-of-course test to equip future teachers with the confidence to recognise and respond to safeguarding concerns — helping schools ensure staff are prepared to protect every child in their care.

What is Safeguarding?

Safeguarding means keeping children safe from harm, abuse, and neglect. By creating secure and supportive environments, it allows pupils to feel protected as they develop in school and beyond, ensuring they have the stability, care, and trust needed to grow and thrive.

Why is Safeguarding Important?

Safeguarding is a crucial responsibility every teacher must be equipped with. At SafeSnap Learning, we make safeguarding simple to understand, so when complex issues arise in the classroom, you’ll know how to respond with confidence. Learning made simple, safeguarding done right.
